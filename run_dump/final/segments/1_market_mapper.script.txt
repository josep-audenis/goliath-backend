Barcelona is a leading European startup hub, with a strong presence of digital innovation, e-commerce, gaming, and mobile companies. The city hosts the annual Mobile World Congress, one of the world's largest technology conferences. With over 1,700 startups, Barcelona is consolidating its position as the first European hub in southern Europe, attracting talent and investors. European venture capital reached €45 billion in 2025, with AI, climate tech, and defense tech showing the strongest growth. Spain's Draft AI Law, approved on March 11, 2025, introduces cross-sectoral requirements for transparency, accountability, fairness, and human oversight, especially for higher-risk AI applications.
